# Stability Circle

For a 2-port network with S-parameter matrix:

$$
S = 
\begin{bmatrix}
S_{11} & S_{12} \\
S_{21} & S_{22}
\end{bmatrix}
$$

The **input** and **output stability circles** are defined by:

### Midpoint

$$
C_i = \frac{S_{ii}^\ast - S_{jj} \,(\det S)^\ast}{|S_{ii}|^2 - |\det S|^2}
$$

### Radius

$$
R_i = \frac{s_{21}s_{12}}{|s_{ii}|^2 - |\det S|^2}
$$

Where $i,j \in \{1,2\}$ and $i \neq j$.

---

# Stability Factor (Rollett's \(k\))

$$
k = \frac{1 - |S_{11}|^2 - |S_{22}|^2 + |\det S|^2}{2 |S_{12}| |S_{21}|}
$$
